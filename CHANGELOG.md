# Trading Logic Changelog

## 2026-07-24 08:10 (Asia/Almaty)

- Added bounded company financial baselines and immutable opening and midday reports with profitability, stability, volatility, closure-risk, dividend, and management indicators.
- Replaced random per-cycle auditor price shocks with evidence-backed two-day audits, five statuses, and next-trading-day decision signals.
- Combined momentum, order flow, industry sentiment, audits, and fundamentals into explicit buy, sell, and wait probabilities shaped by temperament and risk profile.
- Made ordinary passive order offsets directionally symmetric while retaining midpoint execution as an intentional game rule.

- Kept short selling unavailable; sell orders remain limited to owned, uncommitted shares.
